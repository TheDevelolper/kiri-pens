Frequently Asked Questions

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship worldwide. Shipping times vary by location, typically 7-14 business days.

What is your warranty?

All pens come with a lifetime warranty covering manufacturing defects. Nib adjustments are complimentary for the life of the pen.

Do you offer nib customization?

Yes! We offer nib tuning including flex, smoothness adjustments, and specialty grinds. Contact us for options.

How do I care for my pen?

We include care instructions with every pen. Regular cleaning with lukewarm water and occasional pen flush recommended.
